Pomfret Fry






Ingredients:

  • Pomfret fish (2 whole, cleaned and cut into slices)
  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying


Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix the rice flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and salt to form a spice mix.

  2. Coat the pomfret slices evenly with the spice mix.

  3. Heat oil in a deep pan or skillet for frying.

  4. Gently place the seasoned pomfret slices in the hot oil and fry until they turn crispy and golden brown on both sides, about 5-7 minutes per side.

  5. Remove the pomfret slices from the oil and place them on paper towels to remove excess oil.

  6. Serve the pomfret fry hot, garnished with fresh lemon wedges and accompanied by your favorite side dishes.
