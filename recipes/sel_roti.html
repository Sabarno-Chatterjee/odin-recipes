Sel Roti








Ingredients:

  • Rice flour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Cardamom (optional)
  • Oil (for frying)

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine rice flour, water, sugar, and milk to form a thick batter.

  2. Add cardamom for flavor, if desired.

  3. Heat oil in a pan for deep-frying.

  4. Use a funnel or a bottle with a small opening to pour the batter into the hot oil in a circular motion, forming rings.

  5. Fry until the Sel Roti turns golden brown and crisp on the outside.

  6. Remove from the oil and let excess oil drain on a paper towel.

  7. Serve Sel Roti hot, paired with yogurt, pickles, or your favorite sauce.

  8. Enjoy this Nepali delight that brings joy and flavor to special occasions!
