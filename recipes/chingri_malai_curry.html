Chingri Malai Curry








Ingredients:

  • Prawns (Chingri)
  • Coconut milk
  • Mustard paste
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Bay leaves
  • Mustard oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Ghee (clarified butter)

Steps:

  1. Marinate prawns with turmeric powder and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a pan, heat mustard oil. Add bay leaves, sliced ginger, and green chilies.

  3. Add mustard paste, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Cook until the oil separates.

  4. Introduce marinated prawns to the pan and saut√© until they turn pink.

  5. Pour in coconut milk and let it simmer until the prawns are cooked through.

  6. Season with salt and sugar to taste. Finish with a dollop of ghee.

  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice.

  8. Experience the divine blend of flavors in Chingri Malai Curry!
