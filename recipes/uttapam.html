Uttapam






Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and other preferred vegetables for topping
  • Oil for cooking


Steps:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, poha, and fenugreek seeds in water for several hours, then grind them into a smooth batter.

  2. Ferment the batter overnight or until it's slightly bubbly.

  3. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle and ladle a portion of batter onto it, spreading it into a thick, round uttapam.

  4. Top with chopped onions, tomatoes, and green chilies (or your preferred vegetables).

  5. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until the base turns golden brown.

  6. Flip and cook the other side until it's cooked through.

  7. Serve uttapam hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or your favorite accompaniments.
