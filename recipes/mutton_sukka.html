Mutton Sukka








Ingredients:

  • Mutton pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriander powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Grated coconut
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Marinate mutton pieces with coriander powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt.

  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.

  3. Add chopped tomatoes, green chilies, and curry leaves. Cook until tomatoes are soft.

  4. Introduce marinated mutton and cook until the meat is browned and spices are well-coated.

  5. Add grated coconut and continue cooking until the mutton is tender and the masala is roasted.

  6. Adjust seasoning and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

  7. Mutton Sukka is ready to be served, offering a burst of South Indian flavors!
