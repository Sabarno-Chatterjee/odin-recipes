Pound Cake






Ingredients:

  • Unsalted Butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract + Almond Extract
  • Sour Cream
  • Milk
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt


Steps:

  1. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.

  2. Add the eggs, vanilla extract, and almond extract and beat until pale and fluffy, about 4 minutes.

  3. Scrape down the bowl and add half of the dry ingredients to the mixture. Fold together until just mixed.

  4. Add the sour cream and milk and mix together until just combined.

  5. Add the rest of the dry ingredients and fold together until just combined and no streaks of flour remain.

  6. Bake until golden brown and a knife inserted into the center comes out mostly clean!
