Chicken Tikka Masala






Ingredients:

For Marinade:

  • Chicken breasts 3 pcs
  • Plain yogurt 150 gm
  • Red chilli powder 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
  • Garam masala powder 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Ginger paste 1 tsp
  • Garlic paste 1 tsp
  • Mustard oil 2 tbsp

For gravy:

  • Cumin seeds 1 tsp
  • Onion sliced 1 cup
  • Cinnamon sticks 2 pcs
  • Bay leaf 1
  • Cloves 3 pcs
  • Cardamom 3 pcs
  • Red chilli powder 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder 1 tsp
  • Tomato puree 1 tbsp
  • Garlic paste 1 tsp
  • Ginger paste 1 tsp
  • Tomato diced 4 pcs / 1 cup
  • Water as required to form gravy
  • Salt to taste
  • Cream 100 ml
  • Coriander leaves 1/8 cup
  • Ghee /Clarified butter 2 tbsp


Steps:

  1. Wash and pat dry the chicken breasts using a towel. Cut them into bite-size portions.

  2. Transfer the chicken breasts onto a large bowl, add plain yogurt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, ginger paste, garlic paste, mustard oil, and salt to taste. Stir and mix everything well. Marinate the mixture for 1-2 hours.

  3. Grease a baking pan with butter and add the chicken breasts. Bake in the oven for 20-25 mins at 200°C - 395°F until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

  4. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a skillet over medium-high heat, add cumin seeds and sliced onions. Sauté for a couple of minutes until the onions are translucent.

  5. Add raw garam masala: cinnamon sticks, bay leaf, cloves, and cardamom. Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder. Stir and sauté for a couple of minutes, add water as required to sauté.

  6. Add tomato puree, garlic paste, ginger paste, and diced tomatoes. Stir and mix everything well. Add water as required to form gravy.

  7. Season with salt, cover and cook for 10 mins over medium heat. Stir occasionally.

  8. Once the gravy is reached the desired consistency, add the chicken breasts and cream, stir and mix everything.

  9. Add 2 tbsp of ghee (optional).

  10. Garnish with coriander leaves and remove from heat.
