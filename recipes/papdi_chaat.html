Papdi Chaat








Ingredients:

  • Papdi (fried dough wafers)
  • Boiled potatoes, diced
  • Boiled chickpeas
  • Yogurt
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Mint chutney
  • Coriander chutney
  • Chaat masala
  • Cumin powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Sev (crunchy chickpea noodles)

Steps:

  1. Arrange papdi on a serving plate or tray.

  2. Place diced boiled potatoes and boiled chickpeas on top of each papdi.

  3. Dollop yogurt over the potatoes and chickpeas.

  4. Drizzle tamarind chutney, mint chutney, and coriander chutney over the yogurt.

  5. Sprinkle chaat masala, cumin powder, and red chili powder to taste.

  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and sev.

  7. Serve immediately, allowing the flavors to meld, and enjoy the delightful crunch and explosion of flavors!
