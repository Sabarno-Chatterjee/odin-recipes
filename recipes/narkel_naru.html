Narkel Naru






Ingredients:

  • Fresh grated coconut
  • Jaggery
  • Cardamom powder

Narkel Naru with Jaggery, a traditional Bengali sweet, is a delectable variation of coconut ladoos made with the rich and earthy sweetness of jaggery. These bite-sized treats perfectly balance the natural flavor of fresh grated coconut with the deep, caramel notes of jaggery. Often prepared during festivals and special occasions, Narkel Naru with Jaggery adds a touch of warmth and richness to the celebration. The recipe combines the lusciousness of fresh grated coconut with the natural sweetness and earthy undertones of jaggery. The process of making Narkel Naru with Jaggery is a labor of love, ensuring each bite is a sweet embrace of Bengal's culinary heritage. Ingredients:
  • Fresh grated coconut
  • Jaggery
  • Cardamom powder
Preparation:

  1. In a pan, combine fresh grated coconut and grated jaggery.

  2. Place the pan on low heat and stir continuously until the jaggery melts and blends with the coconut.

  3. Add cardamom powder to enhance the aroma and flavor.

  4. Continue stirring until the mixture thickens and starts leaving the sides of the pan.

  5. Allow the mixture to cool until it can be comfortably handled.

  6. Take small portions of the mixture and shape them into round ladoos or balls.

  7. Repeat the process until all the mixture is used to form Narkel Naru with Jaggery.

  8. Your jaggery-infused coconut ladoos are ready to be served and enjoyed!
