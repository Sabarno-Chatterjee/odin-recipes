Honey Chilli Potato






Ingredients:

  • 3-4 medium-sized potatoes, sliced into thin rounds or strips
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 tablespoon oil for stir-frying
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, thinly sliced (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Sesame seeds and chopped spring onions for garnish


Steps:

  1. Begin by mixing all-purpose flour, cornstarch, and salt in a bowl. Coat the potato slices with this mixture, ensuring they are evenly covered.

  2. Heat a generous amount of oil for deep frying. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the coated potato slices and fry them until they turn golden and crisp. Remove excess oil by draining the potatoes on paper towels and set them aside.

  3. In a separate pan, heat a tablespoon of oil and add minced garlic and sliced green chilies. Saut√© for a minute or two until they release their aromatic flavors.

  4. Add honey, soy sauce, and red chili flakes to the pan, stirring well. This combination creates a luscious, sticky sauce with sweet and spicy undertones.

  5. Now, add the crispy potato slices to the sauce. Toss them gently to ensure an even coating of the honey and chili mixture.

  6. Garnish your Honey Chilli Potato with sesame seeds for a delightful crunch and chopped spring onions for a fresh, savory note.

  7. Your Honey Chilli Potato is ready to be savored. It's a visually appealing and flavorsome treat that combines the crispy goodness of fried potatoes with a sweet and spicy twist, making it a favorite among both adults and kids.
