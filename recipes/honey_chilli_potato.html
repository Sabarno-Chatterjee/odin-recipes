Honey Chilli Potato






Ingredients:

  • 3-4 medium-sized potatoes, sliced into thin rounds or strips
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 tablespoon oil for stir-frying
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, thinly sliced (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Sesame seeds and chopped spring onions for garnish
