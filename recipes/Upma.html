Upma








Ingredients:

  • Semolina (Rava/Sooji)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Urad dal (Black gram)
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger, finely chopped
  • Vegetables (carrots, peas, tomatoes), chopped
  • Water
  • Ghee or oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves, green chilies, and chopped ginger. Sauté until mustard seeds splutter.

  2. Add semolina and roast until it turns golden brown and emits a nutty aroma.

  3. Introduce chopped vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.

  4. Pour in water and season with salt. Stir continuously to avoid lumps.

  5. Cover and simmer until the Upma is cooked, and the water is absorbed.

  6. Fluff the Upma with a fork, ensuring a light and fluffy texture.

  7. Serve hot Upma with coconut chutney or your favorite side.

  8. Enjoy this South Indian classic for a nourishing and flavorful meal!
