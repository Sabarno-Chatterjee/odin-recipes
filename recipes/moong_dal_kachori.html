Moong Dal er Kachori








Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Asafoetida (hing)
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Soak moong dal for a few hours and then grind it coarsely.

  2. Prepare a dough using all-purpose flour, water, and a pinch of salt.

  3. In a pan, heat oil, add cumin seeds, asafoetida, green chilies, and grated ginger.

  4. Saut√© the coarsely ground moong dal in the pan, adding coriander powder, garam masala, and salt.

  5. Roll out small portions of the dough into discs, place a spoonful of the moong dal mixture in the center, and seal the edges to form a ball.

  6. Flatten the ball slightly and deep-fry until golden brown and crispy.

  7. Remove from oil and let excess oil drain on a paper towel.

  8. Serve Moong Dal er Kachori hot with tamarind chutney or yogurt.

  9. Indulge in the crispy goodness of these Bengali kachoris!
