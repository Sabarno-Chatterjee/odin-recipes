Brownies








Ingredients:

  • Unsweetened chocolate (or cocoa powder)
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Nuts or chocolate chips (optional)
  • Salt
  • Baking powder (optional)

Steps:

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter together until smooth.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.

  3. Combine the melted chocolate mixture with the egg mixture.

  4. Fold in the flour, salt, and any optional ingredients like nuts or chocolate chips.

  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.

  6. Bake in a preheated oven until a toothpick inserted comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  7. Cool before cutting into squares or rectangles.

  8. Dust with powdered sugar if desired, and enjoy!
