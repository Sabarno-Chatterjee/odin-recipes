Chicken Chettinad






Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig of curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon of tamarind paste
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • Salt to taste


Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with turmeric and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.

  2. Heat oil in a pan and add the mustard seeds. Allow them to splutter.

  3. Add the finely chopped onions and curry leaves. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.

  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil separates.

  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it turns white on all sides.

  6. Add the Chettinad spice mix (a blend of various spices) and sauté for a few minutes.

  7. Pour in the tamarind paste, water, and coconut milk. Mix well.

  8. Cover and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked and the sauce thickens.

  9. Serve hot with rice or Indian bread.
