Coconut Barfi






Ingredients:

  • Fresh grated coconut
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Milk


Steps:

  1. In a pan, heat ghee and add fresh grated coconut. Saut√© until it releases its aroma.

  2. Add sugar to the coconut and stir well. Continue cooking on medium heat.

  3. Once the sugar melts, pour in milk and mix thoroughly. Cook until the mixture thickens.

  4. Introduce the aromatic cardamom powder to enhance the flavor of the Barfi.

  5. Continue stirring until the mixture starts leaving the sides of the pan.

  6. Transfer the mixture to a greased tray and flatten it evenly to set.

  7. Allow it to cool, then cut the set mixture into squares or diamonds.

  8. Your Coconut Barfi is ready to be savored. Enjoy the perfect balance of sweetness and coconut goodness!
