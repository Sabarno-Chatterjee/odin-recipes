Basanti Pulao








Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Turmeric powder
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cardamom pods
  • Cloves
  • Bay leaves
  • Cashews
  • Raisins
  • Green peas
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for about 30 minutes.

  2. In a pot, heat ghee and add whole spices: cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaves.

  3. Add turmeric powder to the ghee, creating a golden base.

  4. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot, stirring gently to coat each grain with the turmeric-infused ghee.

  5. Roast the rice for a couple of minutes, ensuring each grain is well-coated.

  6. Add water, sugar, and salt to the pot. Bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and let it simmer until the rice is cooked.

  7. In a separate pan, fry cashews, raisins, and peas until golden. Garnish the cooked rice with this mixture.

  8. Fluff the rice gently with a fork, mixing in the garnishes.

  9. Basanti Pulao is ready to be served, bringing a burst of color and flavor to your celebratory table!
