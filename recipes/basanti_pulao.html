Basanti Pulao








Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water
  • Ground meat (beef, chicken, or pork)
  • Onions
  • Cabbage (optional)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil (for frying)

Steps:

  1. Prepare a dough using all-purpose flour and water, then roll it out into thin rounds or ovals.

  2. In a bowl, mix ground meat, finely chopped onions, cabbage (if using), ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, salt, and pepper to create the filling.

  3. Place a portion of the filling onto one half of the rolled-out dough and fold the other half over it, sealing the edges to form a pocket.

  4. Deep-fry the stuffed dough pockets in hot oil until they turn golden brown and crispy.

  5. Remove from the oil and let excess oil drain on a paper towel.

  6. Serve Sha Phaley hot, either on its own or with a dipping sauce of your choice.

  7. Experience the delightful combination of textures and flavors in this Tibetan street food!
