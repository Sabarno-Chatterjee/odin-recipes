Chicken Mughlai Paratha






Ingredients:

For filling:

  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 small cinnamon sticks
  • ½ tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 500 g minced chicken
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp roasted cumin
  • ½ tsp red chili
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp onion
  • 1 tsp fresh coriander
  • 1 tsp green chili

For paratha:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour (AP flour)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cup + 2 tbsp water


Steps:

  1. Start off by adding cooking oil in a large pot

  2. Stir in chopped onion, cinnamon sticks, garlic, and ginger paste

  3. Add 500 grams of minced chicken into the pot

  4. Mix in salt, turmeric, roasted cumin, and red chili
