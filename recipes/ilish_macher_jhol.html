Ilish Macher Jhol








Ingredients:

  • Hilsa fish pieces
  • Mustard oil
  • Nigella seeds
  • Green chilies
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Coriander leaves

Steps:

  1. Marinate Hilsa fish pieces with turmeric powder and salt.

  2. Heat mustard oil in a pan, add nigella seeds and green chilies.

  3. Add marinated Hilsa pieces and sear until lightly golden.

  4. Pour in water, adjust salt, and add a pinch of sugar.

  5. Simmer until the fish is cooked through and the curry reaches the desired consistency.

  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

  7. Your Ilish Macher Jhol is ready to be savored with steamed rice!
