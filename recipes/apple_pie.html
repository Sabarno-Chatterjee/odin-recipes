Apple Pie






Ingredients:

  • 6-7 cups of apples, peeled, cored, and sliced (variety such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp is ideal)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 pre-made pie crusts (for the top and bottom crusts)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar (for sprinkling)


Steps:

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples and drizzle them with lemon juice to prevent browning. Mix gently.

  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the sugar, flour, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, and a pinch of salt.

  4. Add the sugar and spice mixture to the apples and toss until the apples are evenly coated.

  5. Line a pie dish with one of the pre-made pie crusts. Pour the apple filling into the pie crust, and dot the top with small pieces of butter.

  6. Place the second pie crust over the apple filling. Seal the edges by crimping them together, and make a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.

  7. For a golden finish, brush the top crust with a beaten egg and sprinkle it with granulated sugar.

  8. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 45-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown, and the filling is bubbling.

  9. Allow the Apple Pie to cool for a while before slicing and serving. It's delightful on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.
