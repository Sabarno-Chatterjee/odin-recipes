Chicken Dumplings






Ingredients:

For filling:

  • 6 chicken breasts
  • 5 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground basil
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sage
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For dough:

  • 5 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • refined oil as required


Steps:

  1. Wash and clean the chicken

    Wash and clean the meat, then take a pan and add in all these ingredients in a large sauce pan to simmer over low heat, until the chicken is done. Then remove the chicken and remove the skin. While the chicken is cooking, make the dumplings.

  2. Knead a dough

    Put flour into a mixing bowl and stir in salt. Work in shortening as you would when making pie crust, using two knives or a pastry cutter. Add egg, working it into the dough. Then stir in cold water gradually, until the dough comes together and is at a stage where you will be able to roll it out.

  3. Cook the dumplings in the broth

    Lightly flour a surface and roll out the dough to a pie shell thickness (about 1/8 inch). Using a cutter, cut the dough into 1/2 to 1 inch squares. Finally, put the squares into the chicken broth and cook until done (about 30 minutes).

  4. Serve hot

    Return the chicken to the soup with the dumplings to cook another 15 minutes or until you have a gravy texture. After you get a gravy texture, it's ready to serve.
