Sabudana Vada






Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Oil
  • Poppy seeds
  • Sesame seeds
  • Dry coconut
  • Black pepper
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt


Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Set aside for some time.

  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.

  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw aroma disappears.

  4. Blend poppy seeds, sesame seeds, and dry coconut into a fine paste.

  5. Add the paste to the pan and cook until the oil separates.

  6. Introduce tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.

  7. Add marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are well-coated with the masala.

  8. Simmer on low heat until the chicken is tender and the gravy thickens.

  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot. Experience the bold and vibrant flavors of Chicken Saoji!
