Idli






Ingredients:

  • 1 cup parboiled rice
  • 1 cup idli rice (or regular rice)
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste


Steps:

  1. Wash and soak the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for several hours or overnight.

  2. Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.

  3. Add salt to the batter and mix well.

  4. Allow the batter to ferment for 8-12 hours or until it rises and becomes slightly bubbly.

  5. Grease idli molds and pour the batter into each mold, then steam the idlis for about 10-12 minutes until they are cooked through.

  6. Remove the idlis from the molds and serve them hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or other preferred accompaniments.

  7. Idlis are not only delicious but also a nutritious and versatile choice for any time of day.
