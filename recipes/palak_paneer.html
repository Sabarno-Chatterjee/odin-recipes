Palak Paneer








Ingredients:

  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Cumin seeds
  • Garam masala
  • Fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  • Cream
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Blanch fresh spinach leaves and puree them to form a smooth green base.

  2. Saut√© onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste in oil or ghee until golden brown.

  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.

  4. Introduce the spinach puree, cumin seeds, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the mixture thickens.

  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer until they absorb the flavors of the curry.

  6. Sprinkle fenugreek leaves and drizzle cream over the Palak Paneer. Mix gently.

  7. Simmer for a few more minutes until the curry reaches the desired consistency.

  8. Garnish with a swirl of fresh cream and serve hot with naan or roti.

  9. Indulge in the creamy goodness of Palak Paneer, a beloved vegetarian classic!
