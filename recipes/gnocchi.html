Gnocchi with Pesto








Ingredients:

  • Gnocchi
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Pine nuts
  • Garlic cloves
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the gnocchi according to package instructions or until they float to the surface.

  2. In a food processor, blend fresh basil, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese until a coarse paste forms.

  3. With the processor running, slowly drizzle in olive oil until the pesto reaches a smooth consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Drain the cooked gnocchi and toss them in the freshly made pesto sauce until well coated.

  5. Serve the Gnocchi with Pesto hot, garnished with additional Parmesan and a few fresh basil leaves.

  6. Experience the simple yet exquisite flavors of this Italian comfort dish!
