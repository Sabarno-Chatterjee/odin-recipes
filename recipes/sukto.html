Shukto






Ingredients:

  • Bitter gourd (karela)
  • Raw banana (kacha kela)
  • Drumsticks (sajne data)
  • Elephant apple (chalta)
  • Radish (mulo)
  • Eggplant (begun)
  • Potato (aloo)
  • Mustard seeds (shorshe)
  • Poppy seeds (posto)
  • Ginger (ada)
  • Turmeric (haldi)
  • Milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Sugar (to balance bitterness)
  • Mustard oil (for a distinct flavor)


Steps:

  1. Start by slicing all the vegetables into thin, long pieces.

  2. Make a paste of mustard seeds, poppy seeds, and ginger with a bit of water.

  3. Heat mustard oil in a pan and add the sliced vegetables.

  4. Saut√© them until slightly tender.

  5. Add the prepared paste and continue to cook.

  6. Add turmeric, salt, and a touch of sugar to balance the bitterness.

  7. Stir in milk to create a creamy consistency.

  8. Simmer until the vegetables are well-cooked but not mushy.

  9. Serve hot as a side dish to enjoy the complex and delightful flavors of Shukto.
