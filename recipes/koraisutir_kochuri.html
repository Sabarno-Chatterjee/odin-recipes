Koraishutir Kochuri








Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Green peas (fresh)
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Asafoetida (hing)
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Oil (for frying)

Steps:

  1. Prepare a soft, smooth dough using all-purpose flour, water, and a pinch of salt. Let it rest.

  2. Boil green peas until soft, then mash them coarsely. Add finely chopped green chilies, grated ginger, cumin seeds, asafoetida, coriander powder, garam masala, sugar, and salt. Mix well.

  3. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them to create discs.

  4. Place a portion of the green pea mixture in the center of each disc, seal the edges, and roll them into round balls.

  5. Heat oil for frying. Deep-fry the stuffed balls until they turn golden brown and crisp.

  6. Remove from oil and let excess oil drain on a paper towel.

  7. Serve Koraishutir Kochuri hot with cholar dal or your favorite side.

  8. Experience the warmth and flavors of this winter-special Bengali treat!
