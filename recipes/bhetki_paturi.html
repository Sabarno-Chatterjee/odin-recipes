Bhetki Paturi








Ingredients:

  • Bhetki fish fillets
  • Mustard paste
  • Turmeric powder
  • Green chilies
  • Yogurt
  • Mustard oil
  • Salt
  • Banana leaves

Steps:

  1. Marinate Bhetki fish fillets with mustard paste, turmeric powder, chopped green chilies, yogurt, mustard oil, and salt.

  2. Cut banana leaves into squares, slightly heat them to make them pliable.

  3. Place a marinated fish fillet on a banana leaf square and fold into a parcel.

  4. Secure the parcel with toothpicks or string, ensuring it is sealed properly.

  5. Steam the banana leaf parcels until the fish is tender and cooked through.

  6. Serve Bhetki Paturi hot, allowing diners to unveil the aromatic delight within.

  7. Experience the unique blend of flavors and textures in this quintessential Bengali dish!
