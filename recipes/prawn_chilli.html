Prawn Chilli






Ingredients:

  • 250 grams of prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 tablespoons oil for stir-frying
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), thinly sliced
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili sauce (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped spring onions and fresh cilantro for garnish


Steps:

  1. Begin by marinating the prawns with cornstarch, red chili powder, and salt. Toss them until evenly coated.

  2. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Once hot, add the marinated prawns and fry them until they turn pink and crispy. Remove excess oil by draining the prawns on paper towels and set them aside.

  3. In a separate pan, heat two tablespoons of oil. Add minced garlic, finely chopped ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until aromatic.

  4. Toss in the finely chopped onions and sliced bell peppers. Sauté until they become tender and slightly caramelized.

  5. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, red chili sauce, vinegar, sugar, and salt to the sautéed vegetables. Stir well to create a flavorful sauce.

  6. Now, introduce the fried prawns into the pan. Gently toss them in the sauce until they are well-coated.

  7. Garnish your Prawn Chilli with freshly chopped spring onions and cilantro. Serve it hot as a visually appealing and flavorful seafood dish that's bound to impress your taste buds.
