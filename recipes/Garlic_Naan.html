Garlic Naan








Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yogurt
  • Water
  • Active dry yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Ghee or butter
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Fresh coriander leaves (optional, for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Activate yeast by combining it with warm water and sugar. Let it sit until frothy.

  2. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, yogurt, salt, and the activated yeast mixture to form a soft dough. Allow it to rise.

  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into an oval or round shape.

  4. In a pan, saut√© minced garlic in ghee or butter until fragrant.

  5. Brush the rolled-out dough with the garlic-infused ghee or butter.

  6. Cook the naan in a preheated oven or tandoor until puffed and golden brown.

  7. Remove from heat and brush with more garlic ghee. Garnish with fresh coriander if desired.

  8. Serve Garlic Naan hot with your favorite curry or enjoy it on its own.

  9. Indulge in the aromatic and garlicky delight of this Indian flatbread!
