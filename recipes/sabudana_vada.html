Sabudana Vada






Ingredients:

  • Sabudana (tapioca pearls)
  • Roasted peanuts
  • Potatoes
  • Green chilies
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander leaves
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying


Steps:

  1. Soak sabudana in water for a few hours until they swell and become soft. Drain excess water.

  2. Coarsely grind roasted peanuts and mix them with the soaked sabudana.

  3. Add mashed potatoes, finely chopped green chilies, cumin seeds, chopped coriander leaves, lemon juice, and salt. Combine all the ingredients to form a dough.

  4. Shape the dough into small patties or vadas.

  5. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.

  6. Gently slide the sabudana vadas into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy.

  7. Remove the vadas from the oil and place them on a paper towel to remove excess oil.

  8. Serve these delicious Sabudana Vadas with chutney or yogurt, and savor the wonderful blend of flavors and textures.
