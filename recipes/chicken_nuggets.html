Chicken Nuggets






Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying (vegetable or canola oil)


Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine the flour, salt, black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Mix well.

  2. In another bowl, beat the eggs until well scrambled.

  3. Dip each piece of chicken into the flour mixture, then into the beaten eggs, and finally into the breadcrumbs, ensuring they are evenly coated.

  4. Heat oil in a deep pan or skillet to around 350°F (175°C).

  5. Carefully place the coated chicken pieces in the hot oil and fry until they are golden brown and the chicken is cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side.

  6. Remove the nuggets from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain any excess oil.

  7. Serve your homemade chicken nuggets with your favorite dipping sauces and enjoy!
